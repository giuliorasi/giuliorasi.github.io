ExLibris Ton Koopman

20 min - 2021
Director Giulio Rasi
Camera Victorie Bonin, Antoine Jörgensen
Editor Lorenzo Petracchi

Ton Koopman is a renown harpsichordist, conductor, and scholar of baroque music. But he has also ammassed in his home near Amsterdam an ubeliveably large collection of rare books. In 2020 he decided to sell the whole collection to a reseach institute in Belgium. This short documentary depicts the last days of a man with the objects of his life-long obsession.



Zamir - Il Principe della Notte

4 min - 2021
Director, editor, camera Giulio Rasi
Camera Davide Pampanin

A short portrait about Zamir, an algerian golf cart mechanic on the island of Stromboli. He shares with us glimpes of his past and questionable tips on how to seduce women.

Produced during the documentary filmmaking masterclass held by Andrea De Sica.



Dennis Sullivan

3 min - 2022
Director Ekaterina Eremenko
Camera Giulio Rasi

Produced by EEFilms for the Norwegian Academy of Science and Letters
